Selected Government Schemes

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana (Residential)
  • Up to ₹78,000 subsidy for a 3 kW system; monthly free electricity up to 300 units.
  • Eligibility: Residential consumers with a valid DISCOM connection; installation via empaneled vendors; grid-connected net-metering.
PM-KUSUM
  • Focus: farmers — subsidies for solar pumps and decentralised plants.
  • Eligibility: farmers or farmer groups with cultivable/barren land.
MNRE Grid-connected Rooftop Programme
  • Central financial assistance: 40% for ≤3 kW; 20% for 3–10 kW (subject to rules).
  • Only MNRE-approved models and empanelled vendors qualify.
State-level Subsidies

Many states provide top-up subsidies and varied net-metering rules — integrate state APIs/portals where available.

How the estimate works (demo)

  1. Take roof area + orientation + PIN code.
  2. Lookup regional average annual irradiance for that PIN (preloaded regional dataset).
  3. Estimate kW installable (usable area × packing factor) and expected annual generation.
  4. Match with central + state subsidies & net-metering rules to produce an eligibility & savings estimate.
